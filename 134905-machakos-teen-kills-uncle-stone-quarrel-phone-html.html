18-year-old boy kills uncle after argument in Machakos

June 2024 · 2 minute read

- An 18-year-old boy has been accused of killing his uncle after he hit him with a stone following an argument

- The two are said to have disagreed over a memory card belonging to the teen's mobile phone, whose contents are not known

Police in Matungulu sub-county, Machakos have launched a manhunt for an 18 year-old who allegedly killed his uncle after a confrontation.

The two, whose names were not revealed by authorities, are said to have disagreed when the uncle declined to give the accused a memory card belonging to his phone.

According to HiviSasa, the uncle had borrowed the memory card, and when the boy later asked for it, he said that it was lost.

An argument ensued, and as the uncle was walking away, the boy, a school-drop out, allegedly picked a stone and hit him at the back of his head.

Kyeleni location chief Dominic Mutua who confirmed the incident, said the man instantly dropped to the ground and died on the spot.

Police who responded to the report found the suspect missing from the scene, and have said that they are investigating the incident and searching for the teen.

The body of the deceased was moved to Donyo Sabuk Nursing Home mortuary.

A few days ago, a pupil aged 10 reportedly committed suicide in their Nairobi home following a clash with his mother over KSh 150.

Police said the boy hanged himself in his bedroom after a quarrel with his mother when he failed to purchase sugar with KSh 150 that he had been given, and instead bought himself some movie DVDs with the cash.
