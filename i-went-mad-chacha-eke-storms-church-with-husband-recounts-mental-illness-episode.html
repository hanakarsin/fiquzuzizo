'I Went Mad' - ChaCha Eke Storms Church With Husband, Recounts Mental Illness Episode

March 2024 · 2 minute read

Nollywood actress, ChaCha Eke seemed to have settled her marital issues with her husband, Austin Faani, months after announcing their separation for the umpteenth time.

Naija News reports that ChaCha alongside her husband stormed the House on the Rock Church in Asaba, today, Sunday, November 27, 2022.

The actress took to her Instagram page to share moments from the event, dancing with some of the team members of her foundation.

The video shared on her page also captured the mother of four and her husband, posing for the camera with some church members.

I Went Mad Just Like In Movie

In another video of the event which surfaced online, ChaCha recounted how she battled mental illness as she literally went insane just like in movies.

The actress noted she had lodged in a hotel with her husband and two children at that time and she suddenly went mad.

According to her, she started throwing away all their property as well as vandalizing the properties in the hotel.

The mother of four said the incident was so intense and what she needed at the time was a trip to the hospital and the husband took her to the hospital

ChaCha added that they were ignorant of the mental illness as they attributed the situation to demonic attack, voodoo, and marine spirit and blamed some family members for being against their marriage.

ChaCha’s Mental Illness

In 2020, Chacha opened up about battling a mental illness after announcing she was leaving her marriage and hinted at being abused by her husband.

She later denied being abused by her husband and said she was suffering from bipolar disorder and it gets worse when she’s pregnant.

Also in June this year, the actress announced again that she was leaving her husband to preserve her life.
