Finland has been forced to turn to outsiders to address its ageing population and workforce shortage.

The European country, which is dubbed the happiest nation on the planet Earth, is suffering from an acute shortage of labour force which is occasioned by greying population.

For the fourth year running, the Scandinavian country has come out on top in the annual World Happiness Report ranking, according to Gallup World Poll released in April 2021.

Finland topped the list, followed by Iceland, Denmark, Switzerland, and the Netherlands, which came in second, third, fourth and fifth position, respectively.

The United States of America moved from from 18th to 19th.
