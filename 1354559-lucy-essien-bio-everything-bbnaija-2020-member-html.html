Lucy Essien bio: Everything we know about the BBNaija 2020 member

February 2024 · 4 minute read

Lucy Essien is a young female entrepreneur who comes from Nigeria. She is among the 20 housemates contesting in the ongoing Big Brother Naija Lockdown (BBNaija) reality show. Over time, she has become a favourite for many fans of the show, who would love to know more about her.

To Lucy, appearing on BBNaija is a lifetime achievement. Like any other contestant, she hopes to win the grand prize, which is N85 million, by the end of week 10.

To this far, her participation in the show has proven worthwhile to many of her fans. Some of them now identify as Lucination on social media.

  • Date of birth: 4th April 1990

  • Birthplace: Lagos, Nigeria

Lucy is, undoubtedly, a new face in the showbiz scene. The beauty was born on 4th April 1990 in Lagos, Nigeria.

Lucy has been an orphan since she was 13 years old. For this reason, she started working hard at a tender age.

For instance, she used to sell roasted plantain on the streets to earn a living – this is according to the information provided by a fan Instagram page created to support her.

Educational background

Lucy Essien is an alumna of the University of Calabar. She pursued Mass Communication at the institution but dropped out of school in her first year.

After some time, she went back to continue with her studies. She eventually graduated with a second class upper. To her, this is her greatest achievement ever. She also cites her not getting pregnant to this day as her other greatest accomplishment.

The grill business and BBNaija

Lucy BBNaija is an entrepreneur. She is the CEO of Lucianas Grill. The grill is popularly known for its tasty foods such as Bolle, fish, and roasted plantain, among many others.

The grill is based in Lagos, Nigeria. Luciana offers its customers a wide range of services. Also, her business offers its services at various events.
