John Travolta Lost Both His Son and His Wife to Disease at Young Ages

May 2024 ¬∑ 4 minute read

John Travolta just lost his wife, which has led some to wonder what the actor's son died of when news of his death broke back in 2009.

John Travolta has gone through a lot of personal tragedy in the course of his life. Just recently, the actor lost his wife, Kelly Preston, after a two-year battle with breast cancer. Before that recent loss, the actor also lost his son Jett, who he had with Kelly in 2009. Now that the news of Kelly's passing has reached the public, many are wondering how the actor's son died at such a young age. 

How did John Travolta's son die?

Jett died in 2009 while on vacation in the Bahamas at Christmas time. After his death, it was reported that Jett had a history of seizures, and a seizure had been his ultimate cause of death. Jett was just 16 at the time. In interviews after his death, the Saturday Night Fever actor said that Jett had also suffered from autism, and had been having seizures all his life, and was diagnosed with Kawasaki disease at the age of 2. 

In the wake of Jett's death, John said that he had relied on his immediate family and the Church of Scientology to keep him working. He also started a non-profit organization in his son's name that was designed to help children with special needs. In the years since it was founded, the Jett Travolta foundation has made donations to the Institutes of Achievement for Human Potential and a variety of other organizations. 

How did Kelly Preston die?

Now, more than a decade after they lost their son, John is also dealing with the loss of his wife, Kelly Preston. Kelly, who was an actor, was best known for her roles in Jerry Maguire and Twins. The two were married in 1991, and she is survived by two children, a daughter Ella, who's 20, and a son Benjamin, who's 9. 

Kelly had been battling breast cancer, but she didn't broadcast that fact. "Choosing to keep her fight private, she had been undergoing medical treatment for some time, supported by her closest family and friends," a family rep told People. "She was a bright, beautiful and loving soul who cared deeply about others and who brought life to everything she touched. Her family asks for your understanding of their need for privacy at this time." 

John and Kelly appeared to have a loving relationship.

John and Kelly were married in September, when she was two months pregnant with Jett. As recently as last September, the two were still posting loving tributes to one another on social media in honor of their 28th wedding anniversary.
