Best songs about unrequited love of the century

Love is not always happy, and it can end in heartbreak and rejection. Sometimes we fall for someone that we cannot have for various reasons. If you are struggling from unreciprocated love and want to listen to some music that relates to this, here is the best selection of songs about unrequited love that were released in the 21st century.

Having feelings for someone who cannot be yours is tough. However, remember that all the musicians who wrote and performed these songs have also struggled with loving someone who doesn't love them back, which indicates that you are not alone in this.

You can listen to the following songs about being in love with someone who doesn't feel the same. Perhaps, you will relate to their pain and conflicting feelings.

Top 10 best songs about unrequited love

Here are some songs about loving someone who doesn't love you back. If this is a familiar feeling for you, these tracks may deeply resonate with your soul.

1. "Say Something" by A Great Big World ft. Christina Aguilera

This hit was released in 2013 and quickly gained popularity. Later, it proceeded to win a Grammy Award for Best Pop Duo/Group Performance. It tells the story of a person loving someone and wanting to let them go, even if it breaks their heart to do so. Combined with an emotional performance from the singers, it is truly one of the best tracks about unreciprocated feelings.

2. "You're Beautiful" by James Blunt

This sweet track tells about a man who accidentally locked eyes with a beautiful woman in a crowded place, and now he cannot stop thinking about her. Eventually, he accepts that they have no chance of being together. This is one of the classic unrequited love songs, as it shows the pain and conflict of the main character who wants to be with his love, but she is with someone else, and he does not even know where to find her.

3. "You Belong With Me" by Taylor Swift

One of the most popular early bangers of Taylor Swift tells us about a schoolgirl who is in love with her friend and is asking him to notice her. The boy of her dreams is dating someone else, who is completely different from the main character: she has a different style and attitude. This sweet and sincere song was nominated for a Grammy Award for Song of the Year, and the music video is definitely worth watching because its cuteness will melt your heart.

4. "Mr Brightside" by The Killers

This popular tune by the American rock band The Killers is not just a catchy jam but also has a compelling story. The lyrics tell us about someone who is in love with a woman, and it is killing him to imagine her intimate moments with another man. However, he is trying to look on the bright side and stop focusing on his jealousy.

5. "Let Her Go" by Passenger

This is one of the most touching unrequited love songs. The character is singing about letting his beloved woman go and only then understanding how much he really valued her. Give it a listen, and you will understand why many people find it so personal.

6. "All I Want" by Kodaline

The band Kodaline is known for their emotional lyrics and music. One of their most popular releases is "All I Want", which deals with the pain of a broken heart. The main character is having a hard time because he still cares about someone who left him, and he questions why this person would say goodbye to him.

7. "Imagination" by Shawn Mendes

Shawn Mendes, in this record, is singing about struggling to confess to someone he has it bad for. The lyrics of this beautiful and melodious song can be familiar to lots of people - they deal with a crush that you want to confess but at the same time are afraid to do it. In the end, all that is left is to imagine a happy life with this person.

8. "Nicest Thing" by Kate Nash

In this song, Kate Nash wishes that her favourite person knew every little thing about her and was there for her through the happy and sad moments. This is something that we all experience when we develop a crush - we wish that our feelings for them are mutual.

9. "Dancing On My Own" by Robyn

This famous track, performed by Robyn, deals with the experience of a woman whose crush is dating someone else, and it breaks her heart to see them together. The sincerity and depth of this song can remind us about our own similar situation. After all, it is quite common to observe someone you love with someone new and wishing it was you in their place. This song also has a beautiful cover version by Calum Scott.

10. "Thinking Of You" by Katy Perry

In this powerful track, Katy Perry sings from a confused woman's point of view. She is in a relationship with someone, but cannot stop thinking about her ex that she still has not moved on from. The lady regrets about letting go and wishes that her love would just come and take her away from her current relationship. The music video, which portrays this story, will move you to tears.
