BBNaija's Nini Splashes N1.6 Million For Her Birthday Dress

July 2024 · 2 minute read

Former Big Brother Naija ‘Shine Ya Eye’ Housemate Nini has reportedly splashed N1.6 million on her birthday dress.

This was disclosed by celebrity stylist Swazzi ahead of Nini’s birthday on May 16th 2022.

The competition to outdo one another is unending among the Nigerian celebrities despite its implication on their finances.

In the post shared on the microblogging platform Twitter, Swazzi said he can’t wait for the world to see the costume birthday dress of Nini.

According to the stylist, the birthday custom-fit is worth N1.6 million.

Considering this is coming a few hours after reality TV star Ifu Ennada revealed that her skull outfit to AMVCA on Saturday night was worth over N5 million.

However, some netizens have expressed mixed feelings over Nini’s post.

While some people claimed that the reality tv star and her stylist could be lying others called on the need to invest wisely rather than following fashion trends.
