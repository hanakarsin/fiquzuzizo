Is Blac Chyna Dating THIS Rapper? What to Know About Her Relationship Status

April 2024 · 4 minute read

Who is Blac Chyna's boyfriend now? Here's what to know about the TV personality's dating life, including her past love affairs. Keep reading.

Content warning: This article contains mentions of domestic abuse.

Although most people are familiar with Blac Chyna (real name: Angela White) due to her old legal squabbles with the Kardashian family, the beauty is actually one of the most successful influencers in the game.

Before a career as an influencer became a thing, Chyna was one of the “it girls” who paved the way for many female entrepreneurs of today. Chyna, who started as a stripper, quickly became a video vixen and graduated to become an entrepreneur.

Chyna created a clothing line, Blac Chyna’s Closet, and a beauty bar and online store, Lashed by Blac Chyna. Since Chyna is known for her beauty and curvaceous figure, finding a special someone has not been too hard for the entrepreneur. In fact, she has a new man in her life, and fans want all the tea.

So, who is Blac Chyna dating now? Here’s what we know.

Blac Chyna is currently dating rapper Twin Hector.

One thing Chyna knows how to do is keep herself a man.

In a now-deleted Instagram post (via Hollywood Life), Chyna confirmed that she is in a relationship with rapper Twin Hector, aka Taiyan Hector. The post included a carousel of images of the pair cuddling with each other on a huge white piano.

Interestingly, rumors about the pair being a couple have been running since 2020. Twin confirmed in September 2021 that he and Chyna are indeed an item in a chat with HotNewHipHop.

“We met in the studio here in L.A., through a friend of a friend,” Twin told the outlet. “Since the day we met, we probably spend about 95 percent of our time together. To this day, that’s my baby.”

Chyna’s mom, Tokyo Toni, was actually the first person to confirm the relationship.

“Today was my first time hearing about it. I’m shocked.” Tokyo told The Sun in July 2021 about the rumors of Chyna and Twin preparing to jump the broom. “She’s with Lil Twin. They’ve been together about two years or so now.”

With that in mind, it appears that Chyna and Twin have been dating since 2019 if Tokyo’s timeline is correct.

Since then, the pair have been posting and deleting photos of each other on Instagram. So, one could assume that the couple is taking a private approach with their coupledom.

Blac Chyna has dated quite a few famous names in the past.

One of Chyna's most public relationships was with Rob Kardashian. The pair had a rocky relationship that grew toxic, from abuse allegations to revenge porn claims.

The pair eventually went their separate ways in 2017 after a year together. The former couple shares 6-year-old Dream Kardashian.

Following Blac Chyna and Rob's split, the mother-of-two had been linked to numerous people in the entertainment industry. Reports surfaced in October 2019 that Chyna was dating rapper Sage the Gemini.

According to Bossip, Sage attended Chyna's son's birthday last year, and when a fan trolled Chyna, the rapper had a few choice words to share.

"One thing that you have to remember is that you do NOT know this woman from a can of paint; please don’t suggest anything of the sort without a clue of what her life is like. Don’t come at her like that no more. God bless you, no hard feelings," he reportedly wrote on social media.

The stars never confirmed their relationship or have posted pictures together on social media.

Prior to reports that the 31-year-old is dating Sage, Chyna was reportedly dating Soulja Boy in February 2019, but the pair allegedly split just two weeks later.

One month prior, the makeup entrepreneur made headlines after she and then-boyfriend Kid Buu got into a physical altercation while away on vacation in Hawaii.

According to TMZ, the ex-couple got into a "heated argument," and the makeup guru accused Kid Buu of cheating.

Their domestic spat became violent, and Chyna allegedly scratched her then-boyfriend. Sources told the outlet that Kid Buu "slammed her against the wall several times and chokes her."

The police were then called to the hotel in Honolulu, but no one was taken to the hospital or seriously hurt. Following the incident, the two called it quits on their short-lived relationship.

Chyna has also reportedly dated rapper YBN Almighty Jay, Playboi Carti, Mechie, Ferrari Tru, Pilot Jones, The Game, Future, and Tyga (who she shares custody of son King Cairo Stevenson, 10).

Now that Blac Chyna has been in a stable relationship with Twin for a few years, it's safe to assume that the entrepreneur may have found the one.
