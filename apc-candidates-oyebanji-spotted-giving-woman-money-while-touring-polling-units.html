APC Candidate, Oyebanji Spotted Giving A Woman Money While Touring Polling Units

March 2024 · 2 minute read

The All Progressives Congress candidate in the Ekiti State governorship election, Biodun Oyebanji has been dragged online after he was spotted giving an aged woman money during the election.

Biodun Oyebanji and his wife Olayemi cast their votes at Okelele ward 06 unit 003 Ikogosi Ekiti, Ekiti West Local Government of the State and toured other polling units.

In the photo that surfaced online, Oyebanji was captured giving an aged woman some Naira notes.

The gesture generated mixed reactions from Nigerians. While some claimed it was vote-buying others said Oyebanji was only helping the woman.
