Early Beginnings – 1986-1989

Ice Cube kicked off his music career in the mid-80s when he joined the hip-hop group called CIA. The group released a single in 1987 titled My Posse. Although they performed together for some time, he left and became a founding member of NWA together with Easy E and Dr Dre.

In 1988, the NWA released a debut album called Straight Outta Compton. It was a hit that sold over 3.5 million copies. The group then released a second album titled Niggaz4Life in 1991, which sold over 2.3 million copies within the USA. But his stay with the NWA was short. He parted ways after earning less than $100,000. Ice Cube decided to launch his solo career.
