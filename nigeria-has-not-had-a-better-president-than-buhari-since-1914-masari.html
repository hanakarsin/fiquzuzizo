Nigeria Has Not Had A Better President Than Buhari Since 1914

The Katsina State Governor, Bello Masari has submitted that President Muhammadu Buhari is the best President Nigeria has ever had since 1914.

He added that no government has performed better than the current administration since the 1914 amalgamation.

Naija News understands Governor Masari made the submission in Katsina at a rally organised by the beneficiaries of the Federal government’s Social Investment Programme (NSIP).

In his words, “Since the amalgamation of Nigeria in 1914, the country has never had a better government and President like Muhammad Buhari.”

He added that President Buhari has done what no other administration has thought of and needs more support and encouragement to perform better.

He said if the NSIP programmes are turned into law, their continuity will be guaranteed beyond the current administration.

“With this initiative which no administration has ever thought of, the President needs more commendation and support to achieve the purpose of the programme.

“I wish to inform you that I am totally in support of this call for the president to make it a law before the end of his tenure. By doing such, it will continue to reduce the challenges Nigerians are facing,” he said.
