McKayla Maroney biography

The artistic gymnast was born on 9th December 1995 to parents Mike and Erin Maroney in Aliso Viejo, California, the United States of America.

She was born in a sporting family as her dad was a Quarterback at Purdue University while her mum was also involved in figure skating. The gymnast has two siblings, Kav and Taryann Maroney.

McKayla Maroney junior career

Nearly every elite gymnast gets an early start in gymnastics, and McKayla Maroney is no different. She grew up watching Tarzan and, as a result, would run around on all fours.

At the age of 2, her mother thought it would be a good idea to enroll McKayla in a gymnastics class. The athlete would go on to compete as a junior from 2009 to 2010.

Gymnast McKayla Maroney was enrolled at an early age in the National Gymnastics Training Center in Aliso Viejo, California.

She has also trained at the Gym-Max Academy of Gymnastics in Costa Mesa and All Olympia Gymnastics Center. She is a trainee of former Soviet gymnast Arthur Akopian and former Bulgarian Olympian Galina Marinova.

McKayla Maroney senior career

She debuted as a senior athlete in 2011, when she competed in the Visa Championships and the 2011 World Artistic Gymnastic World Championships in Tokyo, Japan.
