Kenyans agree Netflix series Bridgerton is better than Maria

August 2024 · 3 minute read

- Kenyans agreed that Bridgerton series on Netflix is better than the local Maria show

- This was after a poll done by TUKO.co.ke on which between the two shows Kenyans love watching

- Some voters said Maria was the best and were supporting local content while others questioned why Maria would be compared to a huge show like Bridgerton

The results are out and turns out, Kenyans agree that Shonda Rhimes's new Netflix film Bridgerton is better than the Kenyan most adored love story, Maria.

The comparison between the two loved shores created a social media storm with ardent fans from both divides giving their reasons for their selection.

A poll done by TUKO.co.ke showed majority of Kenyans found it absurd that the two shows were even on one contrast scale.

One of the reasons given was that the popular Bridgerton film features more hot guys than the local Maria show does.
