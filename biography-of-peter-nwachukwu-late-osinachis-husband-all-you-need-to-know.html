Biography Of Peter Nwachukwu, Late Osinachi's Husband, All You Need To Know

May 2024 · 3 minute read

Peter Nwachukwu is a Nigerian man from Nnewi, Anambra state who is best known to be the husband of late gospel singer Osinachi Nwachukwu.

Real name: Peter Nwachukwu

Age: 50s

Nationality: Nigerian

State of Origin: Anambra state

Occupation: Gospel Minister

Children: 4

Career

Peter Nwachukwau is an Evangelist and a minister of the gospel. He ministers at the Dunamis international church, Pastor Dr Paul Eneche’s church, where he attends with his family.

Personality/Character

Peter Nwachukwu is said to be a lover of God and people, but a ‘control freak’ to the late gospel singer.

Birthday

Peter Nwachukwu was born on July 7th and reportedly in his early 50s.

Marriage/Family

Peter Nwachukwu was married to the late gospel singer, Osinachi Nwachukwu and they had four children together. Three boys and one girl.

Domestic Violence

Mr Peter Nwachukwu, was arrested by security operatives after he was accused of allegedly beating his wife and gospel singer, Osinachi Nwachukwu, to death.

Naija News had earlier reported that the singer was pronounced dead in a hospital at the Federal Capital Territory, Abuja. Earlier reports claimed that the singer had died from throat cancer.

However, different accounts suffered of how Osinachi was physically assaulted by her husband.

Confirming she was indeed murdered by her husband, singer Frank Edward via his social media account recalled one of the times he had a conference call with her and how her husband was jealous of her success.

According to him, a lot of people spoke to her to leave, but she stayed, letting her husband always control which event she goes to minister in and also letting him collect all the money she makes.

“She is always hiding to call people or responding to invitations. I tried my best to help but she keeps saying my husband, my husband, let me talk to him…I wished it didn’t end this way at all” Frank Edward revealed on His Insta story.

Late Osinachi’s sister identified as Favour Made while speaking with Vanguard on Sunday also confirmed she was murdered by disclosing that Osinachi died as a result of a cluster of blood in the chest from the kicking she got from her husband, Peter Nwachukwu.
